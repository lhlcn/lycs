《Dragon Flame Legend》

Dragon Flame Legend is a simple adventure game. The game has no complex operation threshold and numerous daily tasks. You only need fragmented time and simple operations to experience the original and authentic adventure treasure hunting experience. The game does not use a complex data system and explosive combat power numbers to define strength. Equipment and skills start from scratch and transition smoothly, making every piece of equipment have a sense of surprise and improvement when obtained.
